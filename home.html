You're Worth It

Because your time is money!

I believe, that software should be free, but you are more than welcome to show your appreciation, in any way you see fit.

Donate Bitcoins

You live in a culture where every minute counts. From the day you are born to the day you die, you are told that 'time is money', but do you know just how much money? Are you aware of just how much is your day worth? The 'You're Worth It' application aims to make you aware just how expensive your time is. Born out of love of time, it's an ever improving product that helps you realise your true worth and never underestimate yourself again. All of this was inspired by an incredible motivation lecture given by Randy Pausch.

It is incredibly easy to use this application, just input your monthly earnings and expenses and let the system do the rest. In a moment you will know how much money you make per hour, day, even year. All this data can be protected by a four digit PIN code, that can be activated or deactivated at your one will. The project started as a learning experiment but it turned into a real product and I would like to thank: Gaby, Sorin, Sergiu, Teo and Andrei for their help and mostly for their time because, after all everyone's time is money.
