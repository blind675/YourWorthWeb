FAQ

What is 'You're Worth It'?
'You're Worth It' is a simple mobile application that determines your revenue per hour.
Why 'You're Worth It'?
We all know the expression that governs today's society:"Time is money.", but really few of us are actually aware of how much our time is worth.
How to use 'You're Worth It'?
Just input your monthly income and expenses in the Income and Spending dialogue and let the program do the rest.
